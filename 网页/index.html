Research & Development

Innovation Behind the Palette

Every gram of TIMAGE contour powder is the result of rigorous cosmetic science, tailored for Asian complexions.

Micronized Powder Technology

Proprietary milling achieves 5-micron particles — 10× finer than conventional powders — ensuring a silky, blendable texture that disappears into skin without settling into pores.

Asian Skin Tone Database

Over 10,000 Asian skin tones analyzed to develop color formulations that complement warm, neutral, and cool undertones without the ashy cast common in Western contour products.

Film-Forming Complex

A breathable, flexible polymer network that adheres to skin while allowing natural micro-movement. Delivers crease-resistant wear for up to 12 hours in humid conditions.

Clean Beauty Commitment

Paraben-free, sulfate-free, phthalate-free, and 100% cruelty-free. All ingredients are ethically sourced and dermatologist-tested for sensitive skin.

Company Profile

The TIMAGE Story

From a makeup artist's dream to a celebrated Chinese beauty brand.

Tang Yi (唐毅) — one of China's foremost makeup artists with a 20+ year career styling A-list celebrities at Cannes, Venice, and international red carpets. While serving as a guest artist for L'Oréal Paris, he saw the gap: no prestige cosmetics brand truly understood Asian features.

In 2014, he founded TIMAGE to unite traditional Chinese aesthetics with modern cosmetic science, creating products that celebrate the natural, luminous beauty of Asian women.

"Chinese makeup, natural beauty" — this philosophy drives every formula we create.

Today, TIMAGE operates under the Proya Group, with expanded R&D and a growing global presence. The Master Contour Palette won the VOGUE C-Beauty Award in 2024.
