## 1. Bao Wenfei (鲍玟霏) — Team Leader & Homepage Designer

**My name is Bao Wenfei.**

I'm responsible for the overall project coordination and the design of the Homepage. As the team leader, I organized the workflow, assigned tasks based on each member's strengths, and ensured our design vision remained consistent throughout the project.

**This section mainly covers:** the brand introduction and the overall visual tone of the TIMAGE website. The Homepage includes a dramatic hero section featuring the brand's core philosophy — "Sculpt Your Natural Beauty" — with a full-screen background image that immediately conveys the luxury and elegance of the TIMAGE brand. Below the hero, I designed an "About TIMAGE" section that introduces the brand's founder, Tang Yi, and their signature Three-Color Contour Palette. I also included a "Why Choose TIMAGE" features section highlighting three key benefits: Asian skin tone optimization, ultra-fine micronized powder technology, and long-lasting wear. The Homepage sets the aesthetic direction for the entire site, using a refined color palette of deep ink, jade green, and warm gold tones that reflect the brand's Chinese heritage combined with modern luxury.

## 2. Niu Yujin (牛钰瑾) — R&D Page Designer

**My name is Niu Yujin.**

I'm responsible for designing the Research & Development page.

**This section mainly covers:** TIMAGE's cosmetic science innovations. The R&D page opens with a hero section titled "Innovation Driven by Science," featuring a dark gradient background that conveys sophistication and technical expertise. The main content area presents four key research areas: Micronized Powder Technology — where pigments are ground to 5-micron particles for ultra-smooth application; Asian Skin Color Science — based on research of over 10,000 Asian skin tones; Long-Wear Film Complex — a proprietary formula for up to 12 hours of wear; and Clean Beauty Formulation — our commitment to paraben-free, cruelty-free cosmetics. I also included an "R&D by the Numbers" statistics section that visually presents key metrics: 10,000+ skin tones analyzed, 24 months of formula development, and 100% cruelty-free certification. My goal was to present complex technical information in a clean, accessible, and visually engaging way that builds trust with the audience.

## 3. Shi Hanxu (石晗旭) — Products Page Designer

**My name is Shi Hanxu.**

I'm responsible for designing the Our Products page.

**This section mainly covers:** the detailed showcase of the TIMAGE Three-Color Contour Palette. The page features a hero section highlighting that this product is a VOGUE C-Beauty Award Winner, establishing its credibility and prestige. The main product showcase uses a two-column layout with a large product image on one side and detailed product information on the other, including shade options, finish type, weight, and cruelty-free certification. I added interactive color swatches that users can click to explore different shade options — from fair to deep skin tones. Below the main showcase, I designed a "Three Perfectly Paired Shades" section that explains each of the three shades in detail: the A01 Moonlit Highlight with its soft pearl finish, the A02 Shadow Contour with its neutral-cool undertone, and the A03 Warm Bronzer with its subtle golden shimmer. Finally, I created a gallery section showing color swatches of each shade. The design emphasizes elegance and clarity, making the product information easy to digest while maintaining the luxurious brand aesthetic.

## 4. Deng Xiuling (邓秀玲) — Company Profile Page Designer

**My name is Deng Xiuling.**

I'm responsible for designing the Company Profile page.

**This section mainly covers:** the story of TIMAGE as a brand. The page opens with a dramatic hero section titled "The TIMAGE Story," set against a deep navy gradient background that conveys the brand's heritage and sophistication. The main content tells the inspiring story of founder Tang Yi — a world-renowned makeup artist with over 20 years of experience who has created red-carpet looks for A-list celebrities at international film festivals including Cannes. In 2014, he founded TIMAGE with a clear mission: to combine traditional Chinese aesthetics with modern cosmetic innovation. The brand's philosophy is summed up in the phrase "Chinese makeup, natural beauty." I also designed a Milestones timeline section that highlights four key moments in the brand's journey: the founding in 2014, joining the Proya Group in 2020 to expand R&D capabilities, winning the VOGUE C-Beauty Award in 2024, and launching the Summer Palace "Guan Yi" collection in 2024. The page tells the brand story in an inspiring and authentic way, helping visitors connect emotionally with TIMAGE.

## 5. Zhang Xinyuan (张馨元) — After-Sale Service Page Designer

**My name is Zhang Xinyuan.**

I'm responsible for designing the After-Sale Service page.

**This section mainly covers:** our customer service policies and commitments. The page features a warm, earthy-toned hero section titled "Customer Care" with the headline "After-Sale Service," emphasizing that customer satisfaction is our top priority. I designed a six-card service grid layout that presents each service offering clearly: the 30-Day Satisfaction Guarantee with a hassle-free return policy, Dedicated Customer Support available Monday through Friday, Free Shipping & Tracking on orders over ¥199, Quality Assurance with immediate replacement for damaged items, Beauty Consultation for personalized shade recommendations, and our Loyalty Rewards Program with exclusive discounts and birthday surprises. Each card features a distinct icon and a calming jade green accent border to convey trust and reliability. At the bottom of the page, I included a contact information section with the customer service email and hotline number. The overall design uses warm neutral tones and clean layouts to create a sense of reassurance and professionalism.

## 7. Chen Xiangyi (陈湘怡) — Join Us Page Designer

**My name is Chen Xiangyi.**

I'm responsible for designing the Join Us (Careers) page.

**This section mainly covers:** career opportunities and the company culture at TIMAGE. The page features a deep navy hero section with the inspiring headline "Be part of a passionate team dedicated to redefining Chinese beauty on the global stage." The introduction section explains TIMAGE's workplace philosophy — nurturing talent, fostering creativity, and celebrating diversity. Below that, I designed a two-column job positions grid showcasing four current openings: Senior Product Developer in Shanghai, Digital Marketing Specialist in Beijing, Brand Visual Designer in Shanghai, and Customer Experience Manager in Beijing. **My name is Yu Hairui.**

**This section mainly covers:** the introduction of all nine team members and their specific contributions to this project. The Team page opens with a warm, sophisticated hero section featuring the headline "Meet the Team Behind the Project." ## 9. Hong Shengjie (洪胜杰) — QA, Navigation & Footer Designer

**My name is Hong Shengjie.**

I'm responsible for quality assurance, navigation design, and the footer section of the website.

**This section mainly covers:** the site-wide navigation system and the footer with all supporting information. I designed the fixed top navigation bar that allows users to easily switch between all eight pages — Home, R&D, Products, Company, Service, News, Join Us, and Our Team. The navigation features a clean, minimal design that becomes translucent with a blur effect when scrolling, and each page link highlights with an animated underline to show the user's current location. I also implemented the mobile hamburger menu for smaller screens. For the footer, I created a four-column layout that includes the TIMAGE brand description, quick links to all main pages, support resources, and contact information. The footer uses the brand's signature dark ink color with gold accents to create a polished finish to the website. In my quality assurance role, I tested all interactive elements — including navigation links, buttons, color swatches, and scroll animations — to ensure everything works smoothly. I also verified that the page transitions were seamless and that the loading screen provided a polished first-impression experience.
